1. Do NOT Block any member of The Admin Team:

Violation of this rule will result in an automatic ban from the group.
If you have any problem or issue, we'd rather you PM us to resolve it.

2. No Unsolicited PMs:

Do NOT contact a member through unsolicited PMs, whether you are a Giver or Receiver.
Please ask to PM or wait to be invited to send a PM.
Please report unsolicited PMs to the Admin Team.

3. When Posting Item(s):

Your post MUST include the following:

  • photo(s) displaying the item(s) you want to give away
  • a brief description that states the condition of the items
  • the nearest pickup intersections

You also must follow these rules:

Do NOT ask members to PM you directly on your post.
All communication must happen in the Comment Section of your post until you select a recipient.
You may offer your items to anyone you choose. There is no obligation to follow any specific order of comments.
After your item is gone, update your post with WHO your item went to by tagging the member(s).

4. No Crossposting

To keep it fair to our members, items posted here should not be crossposted in other groups.
If there's no interest in your items, you may delete your post and offer them in other groups.
You may also repost your items, as members who missed your post earlier may be interested.

5. When Claiming Item(s):

Limit your claim to TWO posts per day. You may claim all items in a post at the Giver's discretion.
Do not message the Poster unless specifically asked to in the Comment Section.
Comment on the main post, not under individual pictures or other member's comments.
NO selling the items you picked up here for free.
Remember to give as well. When we give back, we create a cycle of both giving and receiving.
