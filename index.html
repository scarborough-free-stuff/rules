Group Rules from the Admin Team

1. Do NOT Block any member of The Admin Team:

Violation of this rule will result in an automatic ban from the group. If you have any problem or issue, we'd rather you PM us to resolve it.

2. No Unsolicited PMs:

Do NOT contact a member through unsolicited PMs, whether you are a Giver or Receiver. Please ask to PM or wait to be invited to send a PM. Please report unsolicited PMs to the Admin Team.

3. When Posting Item(s):

    Your post MUST include ALL of the following:

  • photo(s) displaying the item(s) you want to give away
  • a brief description that states the condition of the items
  • the closest pickup intersections within Scarborough.
  • After your item(s) is gone, update your post with WHO your item went to by tagging the member(s) who picked up your item(s)
  • Please add a manufacture/expiry/best before date for car seats, food, formula, beauty and hygiene products.

In addition, do NOT ask members to PM you directly on your post. All communication must happen in the Comment Section of your post until you select a recipient.You may offer your items to anyone you choose. There is no obligation to follow any specific order of comments.

4. No Crossposting

To keep it fair to our members, items posted here should not be crossposted in other groups. If there's no interest in your items, you may delete your post and offer them in other groups. You may also repost your items, as members who missed your post earlier may be interested.

5. When Claiming Item(s):

Limit your claim to TWO posts per day. You may claim all items in a post at the Giver's discretion. Do not message the Poster unless specifically asked to in the Comment Section. Comment on the main post, not under individual pictures or other member's comments. NO selling the items you picked up here for free. Remember to give as well. When we give back, we create a cycle of both giving and receiving.

6. ISOs and Trading:

Limit your ISO to one per week. ISOs must include your main intersections. Please respond to the members who graciously answered your ISO the Comment Section. Once your ISO has been met, update your post by tagging the member(s) who helped you out. You may offer to buy or trade for brand new items or hard-to-find items.

7. Be Kind And Courteous:

"Please" and "Thank you" go a long way to keep the spirit of giving alive. No rude or insulting comments on Member's posts. Do NOT provoke members/engage in personal attacks. Please respect fellow membersâ€™ personal time and effort by picking up the items at the agreed upon time. If you're no longer interested or can't pickup, please communicate with the Giver.

8. Prohibited Posts:

  • No Prescription Medications
  • No Pets - please use the proper venue to rehome your pets safely.
  • No Groceries or Food ISOs. No asking for monetary help.
  • No Self-Promotions, Spams or Irrelevant links.
  • No Business Promotions or Job Advertisements.

9. Above All, Keep It Transparent And Legal:

To keep this group fully transparent, please do NOT delete your posts, ISOs and comments. This gives us a record of all the activities that transpire in the group. The only exception is when you delete your posts from here and post your items in other groups. Keep everything legal by following the above Group Rules. We reserved the rights to suspend/remove members for: Selling, Unsolicited PMs, Ghosting, No Show, Excessive Taking or False Reporting.
